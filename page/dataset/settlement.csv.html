Settlement Character Assessments for Keynsham

Description: A decision was made that as Keynsham is a large town, character assessment surveys and investigations should be undertaken in the three ward areas of Keynsham (Keynsham East, Keynsham South & Keynsham North). These ward areas were further sub-divided by the Deputy Town Clerk into 108 individual estate areas in which assessments would be carried out.
